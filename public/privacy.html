Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

SimpleFunnel CRM ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SimpleFunnel CRM platform. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you, including:

  • Lead Data: Leads ingested from linked Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertising forms, manually uploaded CSV files, or submitted web contact forms.
  • User Account Details: Name, work email address, and role permissions within your CRM instance.
  • Usage Logs: Action feed history to display team activities, task assignments, and stage conversions.

2. Data Ingestion via Meta APIs

When you link your Meta Account via Facebook Quick Connect:

  • We request authorization tokens solely to retrieve lead data generated by your lead campaigns (via Webhook and retrieval APIs).
  • We do not store your personal Facebook profile data, credentials, or post content.
  • Tokens and page identifiers are stored securely in your private CRM database.

3. Use of Data

SimpleFunnel CRM uses the collected data for the following core purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our lead distribution and management service.
  • To notify team members of incoming leads and automate contact assignments.
  • To compile dashboard statistics and analytics.

4. Data Protection and Security

The security of your data is important to us. All leads, contacts, and tokens are stored in your private database. We implement standard security measures to protect your information, but please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Access

You can view, edit, or delete lead records and team accounts directly from the CRM interface at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@capoasis.com
